# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and respectful environment for everyone, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions and code reviews.
- Focus on the technical merits of contributions.
- Accept feedback gracefully and offer it kindly.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers. All reports will be reviewed and may result in a response deemed appropriate to the circumstances, including removal of contributions or banning from the project.
